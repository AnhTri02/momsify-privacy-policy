Privacy Policy for Momsify

Last updated: October 2025

Overview

Momsify is a Shopify app that helps store owners display product prices with or without VAT (value-added tax) based on customer preferences. The app does not collect, store, or share any personal information from merchants or their customers.

Information We Access

Momsify only accesses store and product data that is necessary to perform price conversions. No customer or order data (such as names, emails, phone numbers, or addresses) is accessed or stored by the app.

Data Storage

Momsify does not store any personal data on external servers. All processing is done securely through Shopify’s API within the merchant’s store environment.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties.

Uninstallation

When the app is uninstalled, any temporary access tokens or cached configuration data are immediately deleted and cannot be recovered.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
